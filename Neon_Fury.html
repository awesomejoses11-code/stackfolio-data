HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS

Movement: Arrow Keys / WASD
Jump: Space / Up Arrow
Attack: J Key
Defend: K Key
Kick / Downslam: L Key
Dash: Shift Key

⚡ ENERGY & COMBOS

Energy bar replaces the old kick charge. Dashes cost 10% energy; Downslam (jump + kick in mid-air) costs 25% and deals 2.5× a normal hit.
Dash attack (dash then attack) deals 1.5× damage.
Energy fully refills over 30 seconds, and the refill time is divided by 1.05 per combo strike — the longer your chain, the faster you recover. Combos also add a small flat energy bonus per hit.
Each successive strike multiplies damage by 1.05×. Combo breaks when you take a hit or stop attacking for ~2s.

💥 SPECIAL ATTACKS

⚡ Speed Punch: Hold Attack (J) for 3 seconds. Costs 10% energy. Deals 1.5× damage.
💨 Drop Kick: Hold Kick (L) for 3 seconds. Costs 10% energy. Deals 1.5× normal kick damage (4x total). Both special attacks apply combo multipliers and can chain with other attacks!
